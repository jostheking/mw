COAL

Coal is a fossil fuel . It is hard and black in colour . Coal is found in deep coal mines under the surface of the earth in the from of coal beds . India , coal is mainly found in Bihar , Jharkhand , Westbengal , Odisha and Madhya Pradesh

COKE

COKE

Coke is a tough , porous and greyish-black substance . It is obtained by the destructive distillation of coal . Coke is almost a pure from of carbon . It contains 98% carbon

COAL TAR

COAL TAR

Coal tar is a black , viscous liquid with an unpleasent smell . It is obtain by destructive distillation of coal .

COAL GAS

COAL GAS

Coal gas is also obtained by the destructive distillation of coal . Coal gas consists of three combustiable gases , namely hydrogen , methane and carbon monoxide .

AMMONIUM COMPOUNDS

AMMONIUM COMPOUNDS

During destructive distillation of coal , ammonium compounds are produced which when dissolved in water from ammoniacal liquor . It is used to manufacture nitromgenous compounds
